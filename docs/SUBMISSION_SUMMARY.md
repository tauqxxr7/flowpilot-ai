# Submission Summary

FlowPilot AI is an AI-powered business workflow orchestration platform for support automation, ticket routing, escalation decisions, and operational analytics.

The project is built around one core idea: business AI should decide what operational step happens next, not only generate a reply.

## What Is Included

- Next.js operations console
- FastAPI workflow backend
- Seeded business policy knowledge
- Query intake and intent detection
- Retrieval-grounded response generation
- Workflow routing and owner assignment
- Ticket persistence and workflow logs
- Dashboard metrics and activity feed
- Deployment notes for Vercel and Render/Railway

## Scoring Alignment

- Model Innovation & Novelty: retrieval plus workflow routing plus operational analytics
- Real-World Applicability: support, sales, refund, onboarding, complaint, and escalation workflows
- Technical Architecture: clean frontend/backend split with API-driven workflow services
- Documentation Clarity: README, screenshots, architecture notes, quickstart, presentation script, and final checklist
